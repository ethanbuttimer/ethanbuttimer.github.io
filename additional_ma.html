Additional Modeling and Animation



Spring 2019 UCBUGG short film by Ethan Buttimer (Project Manager), Alex Zhang (Director), Alejandro Garcia Salas, and Christian Vasquez. This was my first time through the animated film pipeline! I modeled, shaded, and rigged the crocodile chef, the drill, and more in Autodesk Maya. I also arranged the furniture and lighting in the kitchen set, and animated scenes including the jump, the lick, and the flame effects.
