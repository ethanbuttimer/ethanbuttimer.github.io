"The Eggony and the Eggstasy"

Spring 2019 UCBUGG animated short film by Ethan Buttimer, Alex Zhang, Alejandro Garcia Salas, and Christian Vasquez. Created in Maya and Adobe Premier Pro.

First time through the animated film pipeline! I built the main ‘crocodile chef’ model, along with several other assets, including a drill, chef hat, and cheese wedge. I also shaded these assets, rigged the chef, and arranged the furniture and lighting in the kitchen set. Finally, I animated and rendered scenes including the jump, the lick, and the flame effects. As project manager of the short film production, I grew as a leader and developed a passion for animation.
