Geopogo AR

This past August, I joined Geopogo AR as a software engineering intern. We make architectural design more collaborative, cost effective, and accessible by providing an intuitive, multi-user editor and on-site augmented reality visualization.

With Geopogo, I've developed tools and user interface elements for our Unity-based architectural modeling platform. I recently completed a major redesigned of the UI, making commonly used tools more prominent and improving the start menu. I'm also working to give creators the freedom to create complex wall layouts, incorporating procedurally generated curved wall segments.
